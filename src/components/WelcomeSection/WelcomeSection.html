Welcome to the Aadi Sanskriti Platform, which promotes learning and engagement with rich and diverse tribal socio-cultural heritages and life. Our mission is to empower tribal communities through the preservation and promotion of their traditions while fostering their socio-economic development.

Through Aadi Sanskriti, immerse yourself in the world of Aadi Viswavidalaya, our tribally centered online learning hub, where you can learn the intricacies of their arts and crafts from expert tribal artists and artisans. Also get driven into Aadi Sampada, a rich repository of tribal heritage, livelihoods, traditions, and indigenous knowledge, a vibrant testimony to the deep-rooted cultural heritage of the tribal peoples.

Enter into the wonderful world of tribes, learn about them, and learn from them.
